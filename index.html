Project Auto Announcement

This is a device that helps to automize announcements of the public transportations

Fully automated device only needs 3.5mm audio output and 12v power input. Than all the station announcements will be automized with the help of this device. The project is fully open-source details are given in https://github.com/bemregirit/Auto-Announcement-Device . Also you can watch the video below: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9ex4jSiTSgA&t=1s
